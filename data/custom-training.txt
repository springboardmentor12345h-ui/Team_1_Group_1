## About CampusEventHub
CampusEventHub is an **inter-college event management platform** that connects students and colleges for various events.

### Platform Features:
- **Event Discovery**: Browse events like sports competitions, hackathons, cultural fests, workshops, seminars, and tech events
- **Online Registration**: Register for events from multiple colleges
- **Event Management**: College admins can create, edit, and manage events
- **Participant Management**: Approve/reject registrations, manage attendees
- **Feedback System**: Students can submit feedback after events
- **Dashboard Analytics**: View registration statistics and event performance
- **Multi-College Support**: Events from various colleges in one place

### User Types:
1. **Students** - Browse events, register, track registrations, submit feedback
2. **College Admins** - Create events, manage participants, approve registrations, view dashboards

## STUDENT GUIDANCE

### How to Register/Create Account:
1. Visit CampusEventHub website
2. Click on "Sign Up" or "Register" button
3. Select "Student" as your role
4. Enter your details: Name, Email, College Name, Phone Number
5. Create a strong password
6. Verify your email address
7. Complete your profile with additional details
8. You're ready to explore events!

### How to Login:
1. Go to CampusEventHub homepage
2. Click "Login" button
3. Enter your registered email and password
4. Click "Sign In"
5. If you forgot password, click "Forgot Password" to reset

### How to Browse Events:
1. Login to your account
2. Navigate to "Events" or "Browse Events" section
3. Use filters to narrow down: Event Type, College, Date, Location
4. Available event categories: Sports, Hackathons, Cultural Fests, Workshops, Seminars, Tech Events
5. Click on any event card to view full details
6. Check event date, time, venue, registration deadline, and eligibility

### How to Register for an Event:
1. Browse and select an event you want to join
2. Click "Register Now" or "Apply" button
3. Review event details and requirements
4. Fill in required information (if any additional details needed)
5. Accept terms and conditions
6. Click "Submit Registration"
7. You'll receive a confirmation email
8. Track your registration status in "My Registrations"

### How to Track Registrations:
1. Login to your account
2. Go to "My Registrations" or "Dashboard"
3. View all your registered events
4. Check status: Pending, Approved, Rejected
5. View event details and upcoming dates
6. Download tickets/passes for approved registrations

### How to Submit Feedback:
1. Attend the event
2. After event completion, go to "My Registrations"
3. Find the completed event
4. Click "Submit Feedback" or "Rate Event"
5. Provide ratings (1-5 stars)
6. Write your experience and suggestions
7. Submit feedback
8. Your feedback helps improve future events!

## COLLEGE ADMIN GUIDANCE

### How to Create an Event:
1. Login with College Admin credentials
2. Navigate to "Admin Dashboard" or "Manage Events"
3. Click "Create New Event" or "Add Event"
4. Fill in event details:
   - Event Name
   - Event Type (Sports/Hackathon/Cultural/Workshop/etc.)
   - Description
   - Date and Time
   - Venue/Location
   - Registration Deadline
   - Maximum Participants
   - Eligibility Criteria
   - Contact Information
5. Upload event banner/images
6. Set registration requirements
7. Preview and Publish the event
8. Share the event link with students

### How to Manage Participants:
1. Go to Admin Dashboard
2. Select the event you want to manage
3. Click "View Registrations" or "Participants"
4. See list of all registered students
5. View participant details: Name, College, Contact
6. Export participant list if needed
7. Send notifications to participants

### How to Approve/Reject Registrations:
1. Navigate to Admin Dashboard
2. Select your event
3. Go to "Pending Registrations"
4. Review each registration:
   - Check student profile
   - Verify eligibility
   - Review any submitted documents
5. Click "Approve" to accept the participant
6. Click "Reject" to decline (optionally add reason)
7. Student receives notification of status update
8. Approved students can download their event pass

### Dashboard Features:
1. **Overview**: Total events, total registrations, upcoming events
2. **Analytics**: Registration trends, popular event types
3. **Event Management**: Edit, delete, or duplicate events
4. **Participant Reports**: Download registration data
5. **Notifications**: Send announcements to participants
6. **Feedback Review**: View student feedback for your events

## COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

**Q: What is CampusEventHub?**
A: CampusEventHub is an inter-college event management platform where students can discover and register for events from various colleges, and college admins can create and manage these events.

**Q: Is CampusEventHub free to use?**
A: Yes, creating an account and browsing events is free. Some events may have participation fees set by the organizing college.

**Q: Can I register for events from other colleges?**
A: Yes! CampusEventHub allows you to register for events from multiple colleges across the platform.

**Q: How do I know if my registration is approved?**
A: You'll receive an email notification, and you can also check the status in "My Registrations" section.

**Q: Can I cancel my registration?**
A: Yes, go to "My Registrations", find the event, and click "Cancel Registration" before the deadline.

**Q: How do I become a College Admin?**
A: Contact CampusEventHub support with your college verification details. Admin accounts are created after verification.

**Q: What types of events are available?**
A: Sports competitions, Hackathons, Cultural fests, Workshops, Seminars, Tech events, Webinars, Competitions, and more!

**Q: How can I contact event organizers?**
A: Each event listing includes contact details of the organizing college/admin.

**Q: Is my data safe on CampusEventHub?**
A: Yes, we follow strict data protection policies to keep your information secure.
